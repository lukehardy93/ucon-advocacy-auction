Sponsorship Opportunities

Sponsorship fuels UCON's Government Advocacy program — building coalitions, championing local and state ballot measures, and giving union contractors a stronger voice with policymakers. Every tier below helps power the more than $400,000 raised in a single night for that mission.

Sponsorship Window

Sponsorships are open now through Friday, October 16, 2026 — sponsorships close three weeks prior to the event. Please submit your sponsorship request and company logo early to guarantee inclusion in event materials. Questions? Email sponsorship@unitedcontractors.org.

Sponsorship Tiers

Choose Your Level of Impact

All 14 sponsorship tiers below, from our exclusive Arigatō Sponsor down to the Bamboo Benefactor Sponsor. Perks and availability are listed for each.

Every tier funds the same work: UCON's Government Advocacy program, which builds coalitions, drives political support, champions local and state ballot measures, ensures regulatory fairness, and assists members with complex compliance requirements. At $7,500–$10,000, our exclusive tiers put your name at the center of the entire evening in support of that work. At $5,000, our signature tiers align your brand with one of the night's memorable moments — the bar, the photo booth, the stage. And from $4,000 down to $850, every remaining tier keeps your company just as visible to the union contractors and policymakers this advocacy serves.
