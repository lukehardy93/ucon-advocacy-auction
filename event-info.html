Frequently Asked Questions

What time does the event start and end?

The Advocacy Auction runs Saturday, November 7, 2026 from 5:00 PM to 10:00 PM PST. The Cocktail Reception and Silent Auction begin at 5:00 PM, and the formal program runs through the Live Auction, ending with an Afterparty at 10:00 PM. Auction item redemption closes at 10:30 PM. See the full agenda above.

What's included with my ticket?

Your ticket includes the Cocktail Reception with appetizers, Silent Auction bidding, Welcome & Dinner, and the Live Auction, plus the Afterparty. Full pricing and registration details are on the Tickets & Registration page, and the complete run of show is in the agenda above.

Can I get a refund if I can't attend?

Yes, if you cancel in time. Submit a written cancellation request to Rachel at roraa@unitedcontractors.org by October 31, 2026 for a full refund. After October 31, you may still cancel, but refunds are no longer eligible. See the Refund Policy above for the exact wording.

Is there a hotel block for out-of-town guests?

Yes. UCON has a room block at the Claremont Club & Spa, the event venue. The block closes Wednesday, October 28, 2026 with limited rooms available. Book online via Passkey, or call the hotel directly at (510) 843-3000 and mention "UCON 26 Advocacy Auction."

How do I become a sponsor or donate an auction item?

Visit the Sponsorship page for tiers, pricing, and to submit a sponsorship request. To donate an item or experience for the auction, email events@unitedcontractors.org — details are also on the Auction & Giving page.
